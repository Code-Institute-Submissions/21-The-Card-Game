How To Play

The goal of 21 is to total your card values to 21 or as near to 21 as possible without exceeding it. If the total goes over 21 the hand is bust and loses. You also have to beat whatever hand the dealer has. The ideal option is to get your card total to 21 for an instant win!

Start: The cards are dealt to the dealer (computer) and the player (user). Once the initial hand does not exceed 21 the player can then choose to either hit or hold.

Hit: Requests other card.

Hold: Indicates the player is happy with their hand and does not wish to draw another card.

The player can hit as many times as they like once the hand total does not go over 21. When the player holds the dealer then gets a chance to either hit or hold. Once the dealer holds the game outcome is decided.

Card values: Cards 2-10 have their equivalent numeric value. Face cards Jack, Queen & King have a value of 10. Ace cards have a value of 11 or 1.
